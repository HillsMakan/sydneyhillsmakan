# Product Definition

## Vision
The HMG (Hills Makan Group) International website serves as a dynamic, centralized platform to connect food, business, and the community. It acts as a comprehensive directory and discovery tool for regional partners, dining categories, and collaborations.

## Target Audience
- **Food Enthusiasts:** Individuals looking to discover new dining experiences, cuisines, and regional specialties.
- **Local Businesses & Partners:** Restaurants, cafes, and vendors looking to increase their visibility and connect with the community.
- **Community Members:** People interested in local collaborations, events, and community-driven food initiatives.

## Core Value Proposition
- **Comprehensive Discovery:** Easy navigation and full-text search across partners, regions, and cuisine categories.
- **Community Engagement:** Highlighting collaborations and community partnerships.
- **Fast & Responsive:** A static-site generated, highly performant, and mobile-friendly interface with dark mode support.
