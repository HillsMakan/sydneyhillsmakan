# Product Guidelines

## Prose Style
- **Tone:** Professional yet accessible, welcoming, and community-focused.
- **Clarity:** Use simple, direct language. Avoid unnecessary technical jargon.
- **Voice:** Active voice whenever possible. Ensure consistency across all content collections.

## Branding
- **Color Palette:** Professional and clean, emphasizing readability and modern aesthetics.
- **Visuals:** High-quality, appetizing imagery for partners and categories. Use procedural or stylized shapes for placeholders.
- **Consistency:** Maintain a uniform look and feel across the sidebar, hero sections, and content pages.

## User Experience (UX)
- **Navigation:** Prioritize ease of discovery through a consistent sidebar menu and efficient search.
- **Performance:** Maintain near-instant page transitions via static site generation.
- **Accessibility:** Ensure high contrast, readable typography, and proper ARIA labels for an inclusive experience.
- **Responsiveness:** Flawless experience across mobile, tablet, and desktop devices with dark mode support.
