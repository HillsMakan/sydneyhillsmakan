## Meso Beauty Skin Clinic

HMG International Members receive 20% OFF when mentioning HMG International during booking and presenting your HMG e-Member Card upon arrival.

Meso Beauty Skin Clinic is a boutique skin clinic specialising in cosmetic tattooing, advanced beauty services, skin needling, massage therapy, and a wide range of professional skin treatments designed to address various skin conditions.

Conveniently located at St Leonards Square, the clinic is easily accessible by bus, train, or self-drive, with 1.5 hours of free parking available. Meso offers a stylish, spacious, and tranquil environment designed to create a relaxing and comfortable experience for every client. Private treatment spaces are also available, allowing clients to enjoy their treatments in comfort and discretion.

Each client receives a comprehensive skin consultation and assessment to ensure tailored treatments that match their individual needs and desired results.

## About the Founder

Jennifer, the founder of Meso Beauty Skin Clinic, is a highly experienced skin laser therapist and international trainer with over 30 years of experience in the medical aesthetics industry. She has worked with major medical and aesthetic institutions and has successfully treated countless skin concerns across diverse skin types and ethnic backgrounds.

## 30 Years of Cosmetic Experience

With over three decades of cosmetic and skin treatment experience, the team at Meso takes a professional and accomplished approach to skin health and medical aesthetics. Their expertise has helped transform the skin of clients across different ages, skin types, and ethnic backgrounds.

Meso’s services go beyond traditional skin treatments. Each visit combines professional skin therapy with relaxation and comfort, creating an experience that supports both physical skin health and overall well-being.

Their mission is to help clients improve skin concerns through specialised treatments that balance skin care and mental wellness. The goal is to help clients achieve therapeutic results, regain their natural beauty, and rebuild confidence in an effective, practical, and time-efficient way.

At Meso, they believe self-care begins with skilled skin care.

## Our Philosophy

The name Meso represents the idea that “Me is so beautiful.” The clinic focuses on empowering clients to feel confident in their own skin through results-driven treatments and personalised care.

The clinic specialises in improving a wide range of skin concerns, including:

* Pigmentation
* Sagging skin
* Wrinkles
* Large pores
* Acne
* Sensitive skin
* Oily skin

Healthy skin is achieved through a combination of clinical treatments and proper home care routines, helping clients maintain long-term results beyond the clinic.
